Packages

Available package architecture

Six core packages create a progression path from diagnostic-only through to full partnership. This page is designed to be easy to refine as the offer evolves.

01

Foundation

Diagnostic-only entry point
  • Initial qualification review
  • Score interpretation
  • High-level findings and next-step recommendation
Best for lower-complexity opportunities needing clarity before commitment.
02

Clarity

Deeper analysis and prioritisation
  • Constraint identification
  • Priority sequence review
  • Diagnostic workshop summary
Best for teams who know something is wrong but need the right starting point.
03

Alignment

Targeted support around key pillars
  • Focused intervention scope
  • Leadership and team alignment support
  • Short implementation roadmap
Best for operators who need structure without full immersion.
04

Implementation

Hands-on support through change
  • Priority action cadence
  • Execution support
  • Progress checkpoints
Best for higher-complexity businesses that need deeper involvement.
05

Partnership

Full-system reinforcement
  • Multi-pillar stabilisation
  • Ongoing implementation oversight
  • Leadership reinforcement
Best for businesses requiring full partnership and repeatable support.
06

Pre-Acquisition Review

Parallel service stream
  • Business risk and opportunity review
  • Pre-commitment diagnostic insight
  • Bridge into further Axentra engagement
Best for buyers evaluating a business before acquisition.
