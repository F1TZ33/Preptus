Packages

Levels of involvement based on what your business needs

Preptus is designed to meet businesses where they are — from clarity-only diagnostics through to deeper hands-on support.

Level 1

Foundation

Diagnostic clarity for businesses that need an honest view of current pressure points and a written path forward.

Level 2

Stabilise

For businesses that need more than a diagnosis and want targeted support around immediate friction.

Level 3

Optimise

Focused improvement work around execution rhythm, role clarity, and operational structure.

Level 4

Control

Deeper involvement for businesses where inefficiency is persistent and systems need realignment.

Level 5

Embed

Hands-on support to help implementation stick and ensure the business is not left with theory alone.

Level 6

Transform

The highest level of support for businesses needing a more comprehensive reset or rebuild.
