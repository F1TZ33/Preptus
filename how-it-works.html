How it works

A structured way to get the truth without creating resistance

Preptus is designed to surface what is really happening inside a business by looking at operational patterns before decline becomes obvious in the numbers.

Step 1

Capture reality

We gather signals around operational control, inventory behaviour, supplier dynamics, leadership habits, growth pressure, and decision signals.

Step 2

Remove emotion

The process is neutral by design. It looks at setup, systems, and patterns — not personalities.

Step 3

Translate into action

Findings become a practical starting point, with the right level of support attached to the result.

What gets assessed

The operating patterns that matter most

Operations

Role drift, ownership gaps, workflow friction, service delays, and quiet losses in execution reliability.

Inventory & capital behaviour

Stock imbalance, reactive ordering, poor range discipline, and cash tied up in low-value holdings.

Supplier dynamics

Relationship strength, supplier leverage, support quality, and the hidden influence of ordering constraints.

Leadership & growth control

Management habits, training gaps, scaling pressure, and whether growth is being supported by the system underneath it.
