Scoring

Scoring information

The questionnaire and scoring system are designed to qualify clients, identify risks, and determine the appropriate package path. This supports consistent decision-making and strengthens Axentra’s position as a diagnostic authority.

Indicative scoring bands

Score rangeIndicative fit
0–15Foundation / diagnostic-led starting point
16–25Guided Improvement / targeted support
26–35Implementation Intensive / deeper involvement
36–40Full Partnership / high-complexity, high-risk support

What the score is doing

The score creates a structured baseline. It does not replace judgement, but it helps identify where involvement, complexity, urgency, and risk begin to rise.

That gives Axentra a repeatable way to match client need to package architecture while avoiding ad hoc decision-making.
